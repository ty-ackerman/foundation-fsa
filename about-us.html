About us

Our mandate is to break the cycle of students in at-risk communities graduating from high school and not being properly prepared to succeed on their own at post-secondary education

We start connecting with students as early as Grade 8 and work with them through Grade 12 by providing structured after school support in Math, English, and Science. We also provide our kids with a warm meal, their own on-staff guidance counsellors, and the opportunity to attend educational related field trips. Graduating students have access to grants from the Foundation to help assist with first-year college or university fees and related expenses.
